Dance club will developed a creative, fun and easy way of learning how to dance. Dancing will enhance your fun in any social setting. Whether it's a business function, a formal or casual event, a cruise, a wedding, or even a vacation…Let we get you dancing today! Our aim is to teach seemingly complicated dance to faculty and students person in a simple and step by step manner by means of one to one and group instruction , there are opportunities for general practice to enhance and improve your technique. Dance clubs are not just for people who want to become competitive dancers, but also for those who want to meet people of all ages with a similar interest. Dancing is a great way to keep fit, socialise and learn a new skill!

The major objectives of this club are to

1. Study and practice methodologies for translating written text into live performance.
2. Learn the vocabulary and practice methods of respectful critique and evaluation.
3. To strengthen students creative skills in live performance through the study of craft and technique.
4. Develop a multifaceted physicality through training in dance techniques.
5. Integrate an understanding of cultural context into creative and scholarly research.
6. Demonstrate clear and convincing academic writing in the field.

This dance club is to boost up the morale of students and to highlight the talent of dance and creativity. It provides opportunities to dance lovers to prove their talent and it is a good platform for them to have their artistic exploration. This club plays an important role in maintaining the positive environment in the university. By this Club, students get opportunities to represent IIIT-Guwahati in various reputed organizations.
